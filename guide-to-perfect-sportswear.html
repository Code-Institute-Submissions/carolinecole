Guide to Perfect Sportswear

First things first, a high price tag doesnâ€™t always mean it is the best quality. Some high street retailers use the same suppliers and fabrics as the high end sportswear brands for a fraction of the price. High street retailers are able to get the price down due to the volumes they are buying, this reduces the price significantly. One thing high-end sports wear brands do well is the fit and the styling, which is key to good sportswear.

My advise as always is to try both, invest in some sportswear from somewhere like Sweaty Betty or Lulu Lemon for days you want a pick me up, or group training sessions. But I would also buy some for lounging around or going on a run from retailers like M&S, H&M and ASOS - they will not disappoint!
